------ Camera ------

The camera I use is the Canon EOS RP. It was released in 2019 as the entry-level camera into the RF-mount, alongside the Canon EOS R. I bought mine secondhand from kamerastore.com. The specs for the camera include:

  • A fullframe 26 megapixel CMOS sensor,
  • ISO range of 100 - 40 000, expanded up to 50 - 102 000,
  • A digic 8 processor,
  • 5.0 FPS continuous drive
All in a mirrorless SLR style -body.
